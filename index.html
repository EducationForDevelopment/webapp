Instructions for Course Selection:

1. The boxes below link to different "course-finding services", which can help you find the tutorials or univeristy courses that are right for you. Choose one of the top 4 boxes, and click the LOGIN link.

2. The first time you LOGIN to a service website, follow the directions given to complete your registration. After registration, you can login anytime very easily with your E4D emaill address and password.

3. Decide what topic or subject area you want study. For example, "biology" or "mechanics" or "javascript" or "design" or "economics" or "birds" or "airplanes" or.....ANYTHING YOU WANT!! :)

4. To start browsing courses, type your desired topic or subject into the search field at the top of the page. (It is usually labelled as "Search Courses" or "What do you want to learn?" or something similar.)

5. If you have any doubts or difficulty, please ask for assistance from an E4D staff member or another student!
